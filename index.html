Breakthrough Features

Fully engineered to solve the most complex technical, legal, and medical barriers in the field.

Low-Pass Filter (LPF)

Instantly filters mechanical vibrations from potholes, speed bumps, or drop shocks, analyzing broad-spectrum G-force waves to prevent false positives.

🛡️

Zero-Knowledge Encryption

Encrypted locally on-device. Sensitive medical data can only be decrypted using the designated hospital's private key upon actual crash verification.

4-Step Emergency Coordination

Ensuring total data privacy and the fastest clinical response times.

1

Sensing & Filtering

The Low-Pass Filter dynamically excludes engine and road vibrations to isolate actual impacts.

2

Buffer Verification

The SDK triggers a 30-second on-screen safety countdown to avoid false dispatches.

3

Encryption & Routing

4

Clinical Approval

Receiving hospital gets diagnostic insights and dispatches rescue immediately within the golden hour.
